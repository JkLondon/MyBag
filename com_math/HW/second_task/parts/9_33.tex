$$T(x,y) = E T_0 (1 + m \cos (k \frac{x^2 + y^2}{2L}))$$
$$E_{out} = E_0 T_0 + \frac{E_0 m}{2} (e^{ i k \frac{x^2 + y^2}{2L}} + e^{- i k \frac{x^2 + y^2}{2L}})$$

$$k \sin \theta_x - k \frac{x^2 + y^2}{2L} - kL = const$$
